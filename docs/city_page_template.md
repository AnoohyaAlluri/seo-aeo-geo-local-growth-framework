# City Page Template

## Purpose

This document defines a reusable city-page structure for local SEO, AEO, and GEO visibility.

The goal is to make each city page consistent, search-friendly, AI-readable, and conversion-focused.

---

## City Page Objective

Each city page should answer three questions clearly:

1. What service is offered in this city?
2. Why does this city need a localized service approach?
3. What should the visitor do next?

---

## Recommended Page Structure

```text
Hero Section
      ↓
Answer-First Section
      ↓
Local Service Overview
      ↓
Service Process
      ↓
Local Trust or Experience Section
      ↓
FAQ Section
      ↓
Internal Links
      ↓
Conversion CTA
